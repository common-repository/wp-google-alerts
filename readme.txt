WP Google Alerts allows you to view your Google Alerts from inside your Wordpress Dashboard.

== Description ==

This plugin allows you to easily display your Google Alerts on your Wordpress Dashboard.

Once the plugin is uploaded and activated, all you have to do is click through to the settings page and add your Google Alert link.

Track your competitors or simply track who's talking about you on the Internet right from inside your Wordpress site.

Plugin developed by [Christopher Mills](http://www.imod.co.za) with a big thanks to [John A Clarke](http://blog.jc-interactive.co.za) for his assistance with the feed parsing.

If you have any comments or questions, please visit my site @ [http://www.imod.co.za/wpgooglealerts](http://www.imod.co.za/wordpress-plugins)

== Installation ==

Follow the steps below to install the plugin.

1. Upload the `gAlerts.php` file to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to Google Alerts settings to edit your settings.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= My feed isn't updating? =
Wordpress takes time to fetch the external information, please be patient, your Google Alerts will update.

= My feed is empty? =
You will need to wait until an alert is available before anything is displayed, give it some time.
